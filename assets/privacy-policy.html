Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Calorie Tracker ("the App") is a mobile application that helps you log meals and track nutritional intake by analyzing food photos with artificial intelligence. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects and processes only the following data:

  • Food photos — captured via your device camera or selected from your photo library, used solely for AI-powered food recognition.
  • Meal data — food item names, calorie counts, macronutrient values (protein, carbohydrates, fat), and photo references generated from the AI analysis.

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or device identifiers.

2. How Food Photos Are Used

When you scan a meal, the photo is sent to the Google Gemini API for nutritional analysis. Google processes the image to identify food items and estimate their nutritional content. The photo is transmitted directly from your device to Google's servers for this purpose.

Photos are sent only when you actively initiate a scan. They are not uploaded in the background or stored on any server we operate.

Google's handling of data sent to the Gemini API is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and their Gemini API Terms of Service.

3. Local Data Storage

All meal data — including food names, nutritional values, and photo references — is stored locally on your device using an on-device SQLite database. This data never leaves your device (except for photos sent to Google as described above).

We do not operate servers, cloud databases, or any backend infrastructure. We have no ability to access, view, or retrieve your data.

4. No User Accounts

The App does not require or support user accounts, sign-ups, or login of any kind. No identity or authentication data is collected or stored.

5. No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or third-party tracking tools. We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or behavioral data. There are no ads in the App.

6. Camera and Photo Library Permissions

The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library. These permissions are used exclusively for capturing or selecting food photos for nutritional analysis. The App does not access your camera or photos for any other purpose.

7. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service the App communicates with is the Google Gemini API, used for food photo analysis. No data is shared with any other third party. No data is sold, rented, or disclosed to advertisers or data brokers.

8. Data Deletion

You are in full control of your data:

  • Delete individual meals — you can remove any logged meal directly within the App.
  • Delete all data — uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device, including the SQLite database and any cached photos.

Because we do not store data on external servers, there is no additional data to request deletion of.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the App collects no personal information from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
