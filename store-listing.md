Full Description (Google Play / App Store, 4000 chars max)
==========================================================
Track your calories the easy way — just take a photo.

Calorie Tracker uses AI-powered food recognition to analyze your meals in seconds. No more searching through databases or guessing portion sizes. Point your camera at your plate and get a full nutritional breakdown instantly.

Key features:

- AI food recognition — identifies every item on your plate and estimates portion sizes automatically
- Full macro breakdown — calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, and sugar for each meal
- Daily progress rings — see at a glance how you're tracking against your calorie and macro goals
- Meal history — browse all your past meals organized by day
- Customizable goals — set your own daily targets for calories, protein, carbs, fat, and more
- Dark theme — a clean, modern interface that's easy on the eyes

How it works:

1. Snap a photo of your meal using the built-in camera or pick one from your gallery.
2. AI analyzes the image, identifies each food item, and estimates portions.
3. Review your calorie count and full macro breakdown, then save the meal to your daily log.

That's it. Three steps from plate to tracked.

Your daily dashboard shows progress rings for calories and each macronutrient, so you always know where you stand. Scroll down to see every meal you've logged today, or switch to the history tab to review past days.

Privacy-focused: Your meal data is stored locally on your device. No account required, no cloud uploads of your data, no social features. Just straightforward calorie tracking.

Free to use: The app runs on Google Gemini AI (free tier), so there's no subscription, no hidden fees, and no in-app purchases. You get up to 250 scans per day at zero cost.

Whether you're counting calories to lose weight, tracking macros to build muscle, or just want to be more mindful about what you eat, Calorie Tracker makes it simple.

Download now and start tracking in seconds.
